Intro

There, and back again.
It was in the year 2020. Humanity was at the brink of extinction by a new virus, covid19.
Almost every country was in lockdown. Utter boredom struck me, leading to the manifestation of the ubiquitous genome in all of us: curiosity.
"Is playing games all I can do with my laptop the whole day?"
"My creative powers are dying, I must stir and awaken them."
From the back of my mind, arose a question: What is programming?
Think of this as that moment Morpheus presented Neo with the pills. I chose the red one. The journey began, and down the rabbit hole I went.
